The status information is in the form of a string of characters.

ansi x9.13 facts:
  • Amount field is 10 digits bracketed by account symbols.
  • OnUs is logical positions 13 through 32, maximum 19 characters to deal with printing tolerance, usually a blank in position 13.
  • there can be two OnUs symbols in the above section, but expect that to fail
  • The OnUs is to the right of an account number.
  • There may be dash symbols in the OnUs field.
  • The transit/routing field is positions 33 to 43, including the surrounding transit markers
  • the routing field is either 8+check digit or two groups of 4 separated with dash.
  • The one digit EPC field is present if there is a digit between the Auxiliary OnUs and the transit field. It is in position 44 or 45.
  • An Aux OnUs field must be bracketed by OnUs symbols and be to the left of the EPC and routing fields.
